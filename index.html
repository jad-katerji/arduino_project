The Big Question

What if your footsteps could charge a car? What if every time someone walks into a hospital, a mall, or even your school, they could generate electricity that could power something useful? That's exactly what I'm trying to figure out!

The idea

My prediction: If we could turn every step people take into electricity, we could charge a battery and power things like cars. My guess is, if I make a footstep generator, it will charge an Arduino-powered car!

The Approach

I built a car powered by footsteps using an Arduino UNO. When someone steps on a platform, it spins a motor that generates electricity to charge batteries. These batteries run the car, which I control wirelessly with Bluetooth.
